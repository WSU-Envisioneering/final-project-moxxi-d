Courage

Courage, a small pink dog, is kind but very easily frightened. He was abandoned as a puppy after a crazy vet sent his parents to outer space. He was soon found in an alleyway by a woman named Muriel who took him in. Their first meeting is what inspired his name. He lives on a farm in Nowhere with Muriel and her husband Eustace who likes to antagonize Courage. The town of Nowhere is a popular hotspot for many aliens and evil villians that courage protects Muriel from.
